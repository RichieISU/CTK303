Burger King is an American multinational chain of hamburger fast food restaurants. Headquartered in Miami-Dade county Florida. The company was founded in 1953 as Insta Burger King a Jacksonville Florida restaurant chain. After Insta Burger King ran into financial difficulties its two Miami based franchisees, David Edgerton and James McLamore purchased the company in 1959 and renamed it Burger King.
