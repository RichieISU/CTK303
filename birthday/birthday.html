The #1 Song On My Birthday

Nelly - Dilemma ft. Kelly Rowland

Dilemma by Nelly featuring Kelly Rowland peaked at number one on the Billboard Hot 100 on August 17, 2002, where it stayed there for 10 weeks and remained on the chart for 29 weeks. Dilemma received generally favorable reviews from music critics, who praised the duet's emtional balance. The song also topped the charts in the UK, Austrailia, Ireland, and Germany (It Did Not Hit #1 But It Was In The Charts). Dilemma won the an award for Best Rap/Sung Perfomance and was nominated for Record of the Year at the 45th Annual Grammy Awards. Since it was #1 for 10 weeks straight, Dilemma by Nelly, was the #1 song on my birthday. I was born September 17, 2002.
